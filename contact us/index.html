frequently asked questions

How do I get my art listed on your website?
To get your art listed on our website, you will need to create an artist account and submit your artwork. Once your artwork is approved, it will be listed on our website and you will be able to start selling it.
How do I get my art featured on your website?
To get your art featured on our website, you can submit it for consideration. We will review your artwork and if we think it is a good fit for our website, we will feature it.
How do I get in touch with your customer service team?
You can get in touch with our customer service team by email or phone. Our contact information is available on our website.
What is your return policy?
Our return policy is 30 days. If you are not satisfied with your art purchase, you can return it for a full refund within 30 days of the purchase date.
